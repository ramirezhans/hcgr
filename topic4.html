Clothing

Topic #4

Your outfit is self-expression

The way you style your clothes tells a lot about to those around you, thus it's important to dress with intention and purpose. Build your personal brand with your clothing and style. Your style can even reflect your personality or what you stand for. Use your personal style to make yourself stand out and be noticed, as well as to reinforce yourself as a professional person. Clothing gives an idea of your character and even boosts your confidence if you feel good about what you wear. It is essential how you feel in your clothes because this feeling can make or break an outfit. Does your clothes that you wear reflects of your personal 'brand'? Is it time for a wardrobe refresh?

Style is a personal way of dressing or presenting yourself. While fashion is a trend or a manual of dressing, already created by the designers or the fashion icons or the famous people.
