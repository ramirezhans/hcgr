All About Adobe

The multimillion-dollar software company, Adobe, was founded in 1982 by John Warnock and Charles Reschke, two experienced computer scientists working who were working at Xerox. They created a computer graphics software and offered it to Xerox, but the idea was rejected, so they formed their own company and built it up from their garage. Since then, they expanded, made deals with companies like Apple and developed well known software such as Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator as well as creating the PDF Format. They have expanded to many other software industries and offer a subscription-based model for access. These software include uses such as Photo Editing, Drawing, Video Editing, Visual Effects, Web Development and many more. They are now the leading industry in many of these fields and have about 12 million people subscribed to their services around the world.

Creative Cloud is a membership that gives you access to the latest versions of all the Adobe professional creative desktop applications (such as Photoshop, Illustrator, and others) with new features and upgrades as soon as they're available. Creative Cloud is offered as annual or monthly membership.
