Adobe Creative Cloud

Adobe Creative Cloud is a set of applications and services from Adobe Inc.

Adobe Creative Cloud is an application that I use frequently. Having used the Adobe creative products for a number of years. I mainly use Photoshop, Illustrator, Lightroom and Premiere Pro.

Photography

I am dedicated and passionate when it comes to photography and I feel that a big part of that is sharing your photos with your friends, family, and sometimes even strangers in order to get their reaction and feedback.

Clothing

Gaming

I love gaming and would love to design a cool game with amazing graphics. I want to be able to come up with some fresh new idea and really make something new and different than what is out there.
